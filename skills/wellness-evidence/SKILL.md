# Wellness evidence

Use this skill for sleep, activity, nutrition, hydration, recovery, or other
optional wellness streams.

Check the configured availability window before reporting a metric. Preserve
missing and stale metadata; never manufacture a zero, baseline, or trend. Use
simple rollups and explain the comparison window. Separate observation,
interpretation, and hypothesis.

Household correlations are not clinical evidence. Do not diagnose, prescribe,
or recommend medication, feeding, treatment, or safety changes from this
skill. Consequential claims require current authoritative guidance and private
professional escalation.
