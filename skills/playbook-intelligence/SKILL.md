# Playbook intelligence

Use a playbook to encode a repeatable response to a recurring situation. A
playbook should name its trigger, evidence, owner, decision boundary, next
action, and stop condition.

Prefer a small number of evidence-backed playbooks over a large catalog of
generic advice. Track whether a playbook is proposed, approved, active, or
retired. Do not execute a playbook merely because a keyword matched; verify the
source, scope, and approval state first.
